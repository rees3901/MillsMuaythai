MMT Combat Sports School

Join our welcoming Muay Thai classes for ages 12 and up, suitable for all experience levels. Train under Che Mills, former UFC welterweight and Cage Rage British Welterweight Champion, to master authentic techniques in a supportive team environment. Explore the traditional "Art of Eight Limbs" through expert instruction on punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes, complemented by essential clinch work and defensive techniques. Our sessions incorporate technical drills, partner pad work, light controlled sparring, and foundational grappling to build a complete fighter's skillset. Whether seeking fitness, self-defense, or competitive training, you'll develop strength, flexibility, coordination and mental focus in our disciplined yet friendly atmosphere.

About the Gym

MMT Combat Sports School is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment. We are proudly a drug-free zone and promote positive values such as discipline, respect, and perseverance. Whether you're a complete beginner or a competitive fighter, our sessions are designed to develop real skills, build confidence, and foster lifelong fitness in a community-focused setting.

About Che Mills

Head Coach Che Mills is a former professional mixed martial artist and British Welterweight Champion. Known for his explosive striking and technical ability, Che competed in top-level organisations including the UFC and Cage Rage. With years of experience at the highest levels of competition, he brings elite-level instruction and mentoring to every session. Learn more about him on Wikipedia or his UFC profile.
