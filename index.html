About the Gym

MMT Combat Sports School is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment. We are proudly a drug-free zone and promote positive values such as discipline, respect, and perseverance. All members are expected to maintain mutual respect and appropriate conduct at all times.

About Che Mills

Head Coach Che Mills is a former professional mixed martial artist and British Welterweight Champion. He competed in the UFC and Cage Rage, known for his striking expertise and commitment to the sport. Learn more about him on Wikipedia or his UFC profile.
